Edudatt is a mobile app designed to bridge the communication gap, providing an efficient platform for schools and parents to stay connected in real-time.

So why use our platform?
Edudatt offers instant notifications on important school activities, including upcoming events, parent-teacher meetings, assignments, school holidays, and more. Parents receive timely updates, ensuring they never miss crucial information about their child’s academic journey.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Stay updated on school events, announcements, and your child’s progress.
  • Performance Reports: Access report cards and academic performance data at your convenience.
  • Attendance Tracking: Monitor your child’s attendance and ensure they never miss a day of learning.
  • Homework and Assignments: Keep track of all homework, assignments, and deadlines.
  • Know your Teachers: Know your child's teachers name and their experience.
